Welcome to Our Website!

Here at Happy Tails, We try to find homes for those pets who most shelters turn away simply because of their age.Most senior pets who are surrendered to animal shelters end up staying there untill they've passed on, which is absolutely no way to spend their last years! This is why we do our best to give those silver muzzled cuties a second change at family life for their golden years, and of course,catering to their every need untill then.
